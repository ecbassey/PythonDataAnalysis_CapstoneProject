# Impact of the COVID-19 Pandemic on Global Air Travel


### What is the problem you are solving?
The airline industry experienced unprecedented disruption during the COVID-19 pandemic, but the extent, patterns, and recovery of this impact are not clearly quantified. This project aims to analyze and measure how airline travel was affected, and how has the industry recovered over time.


### What tools did you use?
pandas → data cleaning & manipulation
matplotlib / seaborn → visualization
datetime → time-based analysis


### What insights did you or do you want to discover?/ What Solutions do you want to offer? Do People even need these solutions?
This project aims to uncover how the COVID-19 pandemic impacted airline travel by identifying key trends in flight volume, cancellations, and delays over time. Specifically, I want to understand:

- The magnitude of the decline in air travel during the pandemic
- When the industry experienced the most disruption
- How recovery progressed across different periods and airlines
- Whether operational performance (delays and cancellations) improved or worsened


### How would a business or a community (for Social Impact Projects) benefit from your work?
This project helps airlines and airports make better operational decisions by showing how travel demand changed before, during and after COVID-19. It also helps policymakers and communities understand the broader impact of disruptions on mobility and the economy, enabling better planning for future crises.
#### Who benefits?
- Businesses (airlines, airports)
- Community (travelers, policymakers)

#### How do they benefit?
- Better planning
- Better decision-making
- Better preparedness

#### Why does it matter?
- Real-world impact
- Future readiness
